Why choose Computer Science?

The study of computers and computational systems is known as computer science. It is a broad field that includes everything from software algorithms to how software interacts with hardware to how well software is developed and designed. Computer scientists study computer processes and create new software and systems by employing a variety of mathematical algorithms, coding procedures, and expert programming skills.

Why should you take Computer Science?

Artificial intelligence, computer systems and networks, security, database systems, human-computer interaction, vision and graphics, numerical analysis, programming languages, software engineering, bioinformatics, and theory of computing are some of the most important areas of study and careers in computer science.

How much do computer professionals earn?

According to Economic Research Institute, Inc. the average gross salary for a computer scientist in the Philippines is ₱765,151, or $368 per hour. They also receive an average bonus of ₱32,901. Salary estimates are based on salary survey data from employers and anonymous employees in the Philippines. The average salary for an entry-level computer scientist (1-3 years of experience) is ₱540,936. A senior level computer scientist (8+ years of experience, on the other hand, earns an average salary of ₱958,612. According to their compensation data, the estimated salary potential for Computer Scientist will rise 34% over the next five years. Which means the estimated salary in 2027 would be ₱1,023,945 (PHP).

Future opportunities in computing are limitless. Computer science professionals work in almost every industry, performing tasks such as programming, systems analysis, database administration, network architecture, software development, research, and more.
